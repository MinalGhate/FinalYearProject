SSL/TLS Configuration Recommendations

SSL/TLS Protocol Version
Recommendation:
  • Disable SSLv2, SSLv3, TLS 1.0, and TLS 1.1
  • Use TLS 1.2 or TLS 1.3 for secure connections

Grade: GOOD – Servers supporting only TLS 1.2 or 1.3 are secure.

Cipher Suites
Recommendation:
  • Avoid weak ciphers like RC4, DES, 3DES, MD5
  • Enable Forward Secrecy (PFS) using ECDHE or DHE
  • Use strong ciphers with at least 128-bit key length

Grade: GOOD – Use of modern cipher suites ensures security.

Key Size
Recommendation:
  • RSA key size should be at least 2048 bits
  • ECC should have at least 224 bits for strong encryption

Grade: GOOD – Ensures that the keys are large enough to resist attacks.

Certificate Validity Period
Recommendation:
  • Ensure certificates expire no more than 398 days after issuance
  • Renew certificates promptly before expiry

Grade: GOOD – Timely renewal keeps connections secure.

Certificate Authority (CA)
Recommendation:
  • Use certificates from publicly trusted CAs
  • Avoid self-signed certificates for public-facing sites

Grade: GOOD – Publicly trusted CAs ensure proper validation.

Digital Signature Algorithm
Recommendation:
  • Use SHA-256 or better for hashing
  • Avoid weak algorithms like SHA-1 and MD5

Grade: GOOD – Ensures resistance to collision attacks.
